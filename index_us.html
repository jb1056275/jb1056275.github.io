You can make a difference! Help make your city a better place with only 1 click


With Urb you can easily give suggestions on how to improve public spaces in your community or anywhere else in the world and we will go after implementing them.

With Urb you can

Suggest street furniture and other improvements to public spaces in your community or anywhere else in the world.

Check out what other people are suggesting on a map and who's supporting them.

Support and share yours and other peoples' suggestions.

Follow which suggestions have already become a reality.

Urb is a crowdsourcing app that promotes public participation in improving our cities.

After picking a location on the map, you can choose from a list of items what kind of street furniture you think is missing on that spot and what would make it a better place. Once you have submitted your suggestion, we will go after getting it implemented.
